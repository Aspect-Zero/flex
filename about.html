Dr. Anita Viegas

Dr. Anita Viegas (M.D, CMC Vellore) is a Dermatologist and Medical Cosmetologist who has an excellent academic record and experience.

Dr.Anita Viegas had a brilliant academic record during both undergraduate and postgraduate studies.
She stood first in the Final MBBS examination at the prestigious St Johnâ€™s Medical College, Bangalore.


Following this she completed her MD in Dermatology from the world renowned CMC Vellore in 2004.


Publications

Dr. Anita Viegas' M.D. dissertation was on Neurocutaneous Syndromes.
This was the largest study conducted in India on neurocutaneous syndromes; Karyotypic abnormalities in pigmentary mosaicism had also been studied for the first time in India.
This series was presented at the World Congress of dermatologists in July 2004, in Rome.


Her works have also been published in national and international journals and her special interests are Aesthetic Dermatology, Contact Dermatitis, Drug reactions, & Pedriatric Dermatology.


Workplace Experience

Dr. Viegas was associated with the Main Ruby Hall in 2005 & with Ruby Hall Wanawadi since 2015. She has a rich work experience, having been at the Kaya Skin Clinic, Joshi Hospital, Poona Hospital, KEM hospital & taught at the Pravara Medical College,Loni, apart from a busy practice at the Skin Science Clinic, Lullanagar.


Passion and Motives

Dr. Anita's passion for dermatology coupled with her desire to give her patients the very best level of care, motivate Dr. Viegas to keep updating her knowledge & skills. Her commitment to the care & wellbeing of her patients is total.
